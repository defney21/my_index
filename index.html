Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Salt, pepper, and lemon juice

Instructions:

Toast the bread, mash the avocado, spread it evenly, and season to taste. Add a fried egg or cherry tomatoes for extra flavor!
